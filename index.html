platform 01 — about

About me.

I'm a Bachelor of Science in Artificial Intelligence student at SZABIST University, Karachi, currently in my 2nd semester. I'm passionate about exploring how AI and emerging technologies can solve real-world problems, and I enjoy turning ideas into working systems through hands-on projects.

Recently, I worked with a team of five to build RailNova, an AI-powered Railway Management System featuring real-time train tracking, ticket booking, seat reservations, automated scheduling, and an admin dashboard. We presented it at ZAB-E-FEST, which was an incredible learning experience in both technical development and teamwork.

Beyond academics, I actively participate in workshops and webinars on AI, machine learning, and project management to keep expanding my skills. I'm also involved in community service as a Volunteer with Voice of Hope (Karachi Team) and serve as a PTE Pakistan Campus Ambassador, helping students navigate their study-abroad journeys.

I'm always eager to learn, collaborate, and connect with like-minded students, innovators, and professionals in the AI and tech space. Feel free to reach out if you'd like to connect or collaborate!
